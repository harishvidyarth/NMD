NMD Associates is a professional insurance advisory service dedicated to helping individuals and families protect their financial future through the right insurance solutions.

Founded by M. Muthukumaran, NMD Associates brings over 23 years of professional experience across the banking, telecom, and insurance industries. With strong industry knowledge and a customer-focused approach, we guide clients in choosing the most suitable insurance plans based on their needs, budget, and long-term financial goals.

Over the years, we have successfully served 10,000+ customers and currently assist 100+ insurance policyholders with policy selection, servicing, and claim support across Tirunelveli, Tenkasi, and Thoothukudi.
